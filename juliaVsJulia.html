All visualization methods from section M vs. J can be nicely compared using the tool below. After selecting the method for each window and entering the constant $c$ (common for both windows), the Julia set is generated by the Generate button. The comparison of methods is possible in terms of precision, visual look, and also in the term of speed. The speed measurement results in milliseconds are displayed at the bottom of the screen and say how fast the rendering of the method was.
